flutter-accessibility-and-adaptive-design Goal Implements, audits, and enforces accessibility (a11y) and adaptive design standards in Flutter applications. Ensures compliance with WCAG 2 and EN 301 549 by applying proper semantic roles, contrast ratios, tap target sizes, and assistive technology integrations. Constructs adaptive layouts that respond to available screen space and input modalities (touch, mouse, keyboard) without relying on hardware-specific checks or locked orientations.
