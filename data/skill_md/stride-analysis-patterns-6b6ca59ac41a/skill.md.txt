Best Practices Do's Involve stakeholders - Security, dev, and ops perspectives Be systematic - Cover all STRIDE categories Prioritize realistically - Focus on high-impact threats Update regularly - Threat models are living documents Use visual aids - DFDs help communication Don'ts Don't skip categories - Each reveals different threats Don't assume security - Question every component Don't work in isolation - Collaborative modeling is better Don't ignore low-probability - High-impact threats matter Don't stop at identification - Follow through with mitigations
