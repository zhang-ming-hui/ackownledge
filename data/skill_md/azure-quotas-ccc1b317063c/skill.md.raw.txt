Azure Quotas - Service Limits & Capacity Management AUTHORITATIVE GUIDANCE — Follow these instructions exactly for quota management and capacity validation. Overview What are Azure Quotas? Azure quotas (also called service limits) are the maximum number of resources you can deploy in a subscription. Quotas: Prevent accidental over-provisioning Ensure fair resource distribution across Azure Represent available capacity in each region Can be increased (adjustable quotas) or are fixed (non-adjustable) Key Concept: Quotas = Resource Availability If you don't have quota, you cannot deploy resources. Always check quotas when planning deployments or selecting regions.
