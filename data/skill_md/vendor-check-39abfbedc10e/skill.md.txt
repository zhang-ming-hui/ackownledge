/vendor-check -- Vendor Agreement Status If you see unfamiliar placeholders or need to check which tools are connected, see CONNECTORS.md . Check the status of existing agreements with a vendor across all connected systems. Provides a consolidated view of the legal relationship. Important : This command assists with legal workflows but does not provide legal advice. Agreement status reports should be verified against original documents by qualified legal professionals. Invocation /vendor-check [vendor name] If no vendor name is provided, prompt the user to specify which vendor to check. Workflow Step 1: Identify the Vendor
