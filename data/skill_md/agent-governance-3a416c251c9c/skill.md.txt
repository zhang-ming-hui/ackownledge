Agent Governance Patterns Patterns for adding safety, trust, and policy enforcement to AI agent systems. Overview Governance patterns ensure AI agents operate within defined boundaries — controlling which tools they can call, what content they can process, how much they can do, and maintaining accountability through audit trails.
