Automation Workflows Overview As a solopreneur, your time is your most valuable asset. Automation lets you scale without hiring. The goal is simple: automate anything you do more than twice a week that doesn't require creative thinking. This playbook shows you how to identify automation opportunities, design workflows, and implement them without writing code. Step 1: Identify What to Automate Not every task should be automated. Start by finding the highest-value opportunities. Automation audit (spend 1 hour on this): Track every task you do for a week (use a notebook or simple spreadsheet) For each task, note: How long it takes How often you do it (daily, weekly, monthly) Whether it's repetitive or requires judgment
