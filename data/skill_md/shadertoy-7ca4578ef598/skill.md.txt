Shadertoy Shader Development Overview Shadertoy is a platform for creating and sharing GLSL fragment shaders that run in the browser using WebGL. This skill provides comprehensive guidance for writing shaders including GLSL ES syntax, common patterns, mathematical techniques, and best practices specific to real-time procedural graphics.
