References references/get-started.md (install + app integration + sign-in flow) references/cli-examples.md (real op examples) Workflow Check OS + shell. Verify CLI present: op --version . Confirm desktop app integration is enabled (per get-started) and the app is unlocked. REQUIRED: create a fresh tmux session for all op commands (no direct op calls outside tmux). Sign in / authorize inside tmux: op signin (expect app prompt). Verify access inside tmux: op whoami (must succeed before any secret read). If multiple accounts: use --account or OP_ACCOUNT .
