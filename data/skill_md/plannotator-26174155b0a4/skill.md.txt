plannotator — Interactive Plan & Diff Review Keyword: plan | Source: https://github.com/backnotprop/plannotator Annotate and review AI coding agent plans visually, share with your team, send feedback with one click. Works with Claude Code , OpenCode , Gemini CLI , and Codex CLI .
