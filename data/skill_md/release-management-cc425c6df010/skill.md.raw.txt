Music Release Management Manage music release campaigns using RELEASE.md as the single source of truth. Folder Structure Releases live inside an artist workspace under releases/ . The full path from the sandbox root: orgs/{org}/ └── artists/ └── {artist-slug}/ └── releases/ └── {release-slug}/ └── RELEASE.md Use lowercase-kebab-case for release slugs (e.g. blue-slide-park , debut-ep ).
